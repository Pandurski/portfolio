Career Journey

Igor Pandurski, MBA, OCP — Director of AI and Software Engineering

I've spent over twenty years building enterprise software across four continents. Led teams of up to 15 engineers, managed multi-million-dollar programs, and built products showcased on the biggest stages. Directed 8+ concurrent integration programs during a financial crisis while client institutions were collapsing around us.

My path has never been conventional. I started as a military engineering officer, moved into software development, then into project management for global organizations, and eventually into senior technical architecture roles at Oracle. Along the way I earned certifications, pursued graduate studies, and kept building.

Key Milestones

Engineering Officer (Lt.), Platoon Commander

2000 – 2002 · Ministry of Defence, Skopje, North Macedonia

Commissioned officer. Led an engineering platoon of 30+ soldiers. Full operational responsibility: training, field operations, logistics, equipment, and readiness. Where discipline and leadership became second nature.

Software Engineer → Sr. Software Engineer

2002 – 2008 · QBE Insurance, Skopje & Tallinn, Estonia

Six years building core insurance systems in Oracle Forms and PL/SQL. Promoted to Senior Software Engineer for leadership in critical system delivery. Led development of mission-critical modules across two countries. Completed graduate coursework in Computer Science with a 4.0 GPA in parallel.

Project/Program Manager → Technical Analyst → Sr. Solutions Architect

2008 – 2018 · Seavus, Thomson Reuters, Hatch, Accenture, Talan (US & Canada)

Promoted from Project Manager to Program Manager at Seavus upon relocation to the U.S. Managed an organization of 52 people including two PMs reporting to me. Directed 8+ concurrent integration programs during the 2008 financial crisis while client institutions were failing. Brought in-house by Thomson Reuters as a permanent hire in Canada. Continued through enterprise roles at Hatch, Accenture, and Talan, managing multi-million-dollar programs and Fortune 500 client engagements.

Sr. Software Analyst → Sr. Principal Engineer

2018 – 2024 · Oracle, Ontario

Six years, three promotions (IC4 to IC5). Led a team of 15 engineers across platform modernization and cloud-native product initiatives. From Sr. Software Analyst in North American Applications to Sr. Application Architect in Global Revenue Operations to Sr. Principal Engineer at Oracle Health. Solutions showcased at Oracle CloudWorld to thousands of enterprise customers. Left this role to found Geahsoft.

Director of AI & SWE → Founder

2024 – Present · Geahsoft & Applicora, Ontario, Canada

Left a senior role at Oracle to complete my MBA and start Geahsoft, an AI-first consulting and software development practice. Consulting work revealed problems deep enough to build products around, leading to the creation of Applicora and Agentino. In 2025, launched Applicora as a standalone company.

What Emerged
Applicora is a career and talent AI platform with a proprietary 14-dimension matching algorithm, redefining how job seekers and employers find each other in a market that's broken on both sides.
Agentino is an AI-powered automation platform for service businesses that turns every completed job into repeat revenue through intelligent follow-ups, reviews, referrals, and rebookings.

Looking Ahead

Every role taught me something. The military taught me discipline and accountability. Development taught me to think in systems. Project management taught me to deliver under pressure. Architecture taught me to see the bigger picture. And building my own company taught me that real problems are the only ones worth solving.

I build software that works, lead teams that deliver, and focus on problems that matter. If something here resonates, let's talk.
