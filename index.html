About Me

I’ve grown from a hands-on Software Engineer into a Software Development Manager over the past two decades, progressively taking on roles in development, architecture, project leadership, and technical management. My journey started as a Second Lieutenant and Engineering Officer in the Army, where I led teams building GIS systems for mission-critical operations—an experience that laid the foundation for my leadership style and operational discipline. Since then, I’ve delivered complex, high-impact systems across a range of enterprise and consulting environments.


I focus on building high-performing teams, fostering cross-functional collaboration, and aligning technical execution with business goals. I’ve successfully led distributed global teams, scaled agile practices, and managed full product lifecycles—from initial planning through release and ongoing optimization.


Recognized for bridging the gap between engineering and strategy, I bring clarity to complex initiatives, guide teams toward autonomy, and foster cultures where innovation, accountability, and technical excellence thrive. I'm driven by a passion for purposeful leadership, operational rigor, and delivering meaningful, measurable results.
